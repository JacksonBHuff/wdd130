Hi y'all, I am from Spanish Fork, UT. I am a Computer Science Major, Minoring in Electrical Engineering. I have been married to the love of my life, Hannah, for just over a year. We got married after 1 year of dating and 7 years of friendship. We are currently living in Rexburg with our little kitty named Misty. Hannah will be graduating in about a year at which time I will go to in-person classes again and work towards my own graduation.

I am currently employed full-time at Basic American Foods, a potato processing factory, where I work as a supervisor over a line that packages hashbrowns. I have been working there for a year and doing as much school as I can in the mean time. I don't plan to be a potato worker forever, although I'm still not entirely sure what I wanna be when I grow up, I just know that I have a fascination and natural inclination for engineering and computers.

That experience is what began my personal conversion to Christs restored church. This lead to me serving a full-time mission when I turned 18 in the Little Rock Arkansas Mission.
