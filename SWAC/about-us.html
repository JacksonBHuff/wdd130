About Us

Why We Do What We Do

The Utah County Shop with a Cop program was founded by officers, retail workers, and a handful of volunteers on the principles of fostering trust, happiness, and a sense of relationship between children and law enforcement officers. Many of the families we serve face economic challenges and may have encountered law enforcement in less-than-positive situations due to some of those hardships. By connecting local youth with police officers in a fun, caring environment, we aim to reshape how children see law enforcement.

Our mission is to foster trust, compassion, and understanding, paving the way for positive community relationships for years to come.

Why Choose Utah County Shop with a Cop?

Shop with a Cop Utah County is not just an event—it is a tradition that unites a community and a chance to change lives. Unlike other events, our program fosters meaningful, long-term change by addressing the unique needs of children and families in our area. We’re committed to building a supportive network between the community and law enforcement, and our hands-on, locally focused approach ensures every dollar and effort directly benefits children in Utah County.

How the Day Unfolds

On the day of the event, parents bring their children to the Spanish Fork Walmart early in the morning. Every child is paired with a police officer, and they start the day riding in police cruisers around town to view Christmas lights. This time is filled with laughter and excitement as children bond with officers and ask them questions.

Breakfast

After the tour, everyone gathers at a local school cafeteria for breakfast. This shared meal gives children a chance to meet other kids and feel more connected to their officers, making the experience more friendly and inviting.

Shopping

After breakfast, everyone heads back to Walmart. Each child receives a prepaid card loaded with donated funds, giving them the opportunity to shop for holiday gifts. This experience allows the children to choose presents for themselves and their loved ones, creating a sense of pride and joy that embodies the true spirit of the holiday season.
